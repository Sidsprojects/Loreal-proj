Your 30 Day Color lock Plan

Hi Cutie! Don’t be dukhi about your fading color! Keep your shade glossy, shiny, and selfie-ready.

Your Daily/Weekly Routine:

    Double Shampoo

  • Wash twice with Vitamino Color Spectrum Shampoo → 1st wash removes impurities, 2nd wash hugs your color.
  • Twice the shampoo, twice the shine, zero dull vibes.

    Mask Time = Me Time

  • Apply Vitamino Mask, leave for 5 mins (scroll Insta, do a selfie, thank us later).
  • It’s like a spa facial, but for your hair color

    Glass Shine Serum

  • Apply serum on towel dried hair. Activate with heat either by blow dry or a straightening iron for instant shine.
  • Think of it as your color’s bodyguard.

    Blow Dry & Taddaaa

  • Blow dry or style as usual → notice your color shining brighter than your ex’s future 😉.

Weekly Check-In:

✨ Once a week, replace mask time with an extra pamper session (steam towel wrap for 5 mins = pro trick).

Bouncy 30-Day Curl Care Plan

Hola curl queen! Frizz, who? Make your curls the main character in every selfie!

Your Daily/Weekly Routine:

    Double Shampoo

  • 1st wash = clears scalp, 2nd wash = preps curls for hydration.
  • Two washes = zero buildup, hello juicy curls.

    Mask Time = Curl Hug

  • Apply Curl Expression Mask → leave 5 mins → let your curls sip hydration like iced coffee on a hot day.
  • More bounce, less poof.

    Leave-In Cream

  • On damp hair, apply Curl Expression Creamsection by section.
  • Because every curl deserves VIP treatment.

    Styling Secret

  • Air dry or diffuse low heat. Scrunch once dry to break the cast.
  • Soft curls, not stiff curls. Big yes ✅.

Refresh Days (Day 2/3):

a pea-size of styler (Leave-In Cream) → scrunch → DONE!.

Because curls should live longer than your eyeliner.

30-Day Promise:

By Day 30, expect compliments like: Are those your natural curls? (wink and say yesss 💅).
